⚙️ Token Mechanics & Circulation Loop

📥 70% Play-to-Earn

70% of tokens spent on store item purchases are recycled straight back to the play-to-earn pool, creating a circular loop that extends emission lifetime indefinitely.

🥩 20% Staking Yield

20% of store sales are sent directly to the Staking contract as rewards, funding a sustainable APY for users who lock their tokens.

💻 10% Dev Allocation

10% of store sales route to the developer treasury wallet to fund ongoing code enhancements, server operations, and infrastructure scaling.
