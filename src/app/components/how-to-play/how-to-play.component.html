🧠 How to Play BrainBook

Welcome to the ultimate Web3 play-to-earn trivia game! Put your knowledge to the test, climb the leaderboard, earn ERC-20 tokens, and collect exclusive milestone achievements on Celo & Base.

🔌

1. Connect Wallet

Connect your Web3 Wallet (like MetaMask or MiniPay) to authorize payouts and load your blockchain inventory of badges & items.

📚

2. Choose Category

Select from over 22 unique categories, including History, Geography, Pop Culture, and Math. Play standard rounds or aim to complete the whole topic!

3. Answer & Earn

Answer questions correctly before the timer runs out! Earn 0.1 $BRAINBOOK per question, plus a massive 5.0 token bonus for completing a topic.

🎮 Gameplay Mechanics

  • ❤️ 3 Lives Limit: Each wrong answer or timed-out question costs you 1 life. Lose all 3 and the round ends!
  • 🔥 Streak Multiplier: Answer consecutive questions correctly to boost your score:
    • 5+ Streak: 2x Score Multiplier
    • 10+ Streak: 3x Score Multiplier
  • ⚡ Speed Bonus: Answer in the first half of the timer to receive up to 50% extra score points!

💡 Pro Tip: Use Game Items

Buy power-ups in the Store using your earned tokens to help you survive tough questions:

  • ⏳ Time Freeze (5 tokens): Pauses the timer for 5 seconds.
  • 💡 Hint Reveal (3 tokens): Reveals a text hint.
  • ❌ Eliminate Option (4 tokens): Deletes one incorrect choice.
